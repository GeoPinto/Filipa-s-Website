ABOUT ME

Having a very independent character, I started working when I was sixteen years old, so I would not depend on anyone to have money for my expenses. And I started in sales.
Following my passion for science and nature, I graduated in Applied Geosciences in 2005, although I had already worked during college years in Zara, always related to sales.
After my Bachelor in Science, I decided to focus on the wellness and beauty industry, another passion of mine, and I graduated in Cosmetology and Spa Management, already working in the industry at the time.
I started working in offline sales and marketing companies, until I shifted for the online (e-commerce) market. I realized my passion in working with different fields, different people, different tools, learning everyday, with one thing in common: helping people to develop and grow their business with my help.
